# Privacy Policy for Let’s Go Pilot

**Effective Date:** April 2026

Let’s Go Pilot (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application designed for drivers and service providers.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of information:

- **Personal Information:** Name, phone number, email address  
- **Driver Information:** Vehicle details, driving license information, profile photo  
- **Location Data:** Real-time and background location for ride matching and navigation  
- **Usage Data:** Device information, app activity, and log data  
- **Earnings Information:** Ride history, trip details, and earnings records  

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:

- Enable drivers to offer and manage rides  
- Match drivers with passengers based on routes and schedules  
- Facilitate communication between drivers and passengers  
- Track rides and calculate earnings  
- Improve app performance and user experience  
- Ensure safety, security, and fraud prevention  

---

## 3. Location Data Usage
Let’s Go Pilot uses your location to:

- Provide accurate pickup and drop-off points  
- Match you with nearby passengers  
- Enable real-time ride tracking and navigation  

Location data is only collected with your permission and used strictly for app functionality.

---

## 4. Sharing of Information
We do not sell your personal data. We may share information:

- With passengers (limited to necessary ride details such as name, vehicle, and contact info)  
- With service providers (e.g., maps, analytics tools) to support app functionality  
- If required by law or to protect safety and rights  

---

## 5. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices
You have the right to:

- Access, update, or delete your personal information  
- Disable location access through device settings  
- Request account deletion at any time  

---

## 7. Data Retention
We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

---

## 8. Third-Party Services
Let’s Go Pilot may use third-party services such as mapping, analytics, or payment providers. These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

---

## 9. Children’s Privacy
Let’s Go Pilot is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be notified within the app or through other communication channels.

---

## 11. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**Email:** letsgoisdp@gmail.com  

---
