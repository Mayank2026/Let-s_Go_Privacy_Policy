**Privacy Policy for Let’sGo**

Effective Date: April 2026

Let’sGo (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

**1. Information We Collect**
We may collect the following types of information:

* Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address
* Location Data: Real-time and background location for ride matching and navigation
* Profile Information: Profile photo, preferences, and ratings
* Usage Data: App interactions, device information, and log data

**2. How We Use Your Information**
We use the collected information to:

* Provide and improve our ride-sharing services
* Match users based on routes and schedules
* Enable communication between drivers and passengers
* Ensure safety, security, and fraud prevention
* Send notifications and updates related to rides

**3. Location Data Usage**
Let’sGo uses your location to:

* Find nearby rides and users
* Provide accurate pickup and drop-off points
* Improve route matching and user experience

Location data is only used when necessary and with your permission.

**4. Sharing of Information**
We do not sell your personal data. We may share information:

* With other users (limited to necessary ride details)

**5. Data Security**
We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

**6. Your Rights and Choices**
You have the right to:

* Access, update, or delete your personal data
* Disable location services anytime
* Request account deletion

**7. Data Retention**
We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

**8. Third-Party Services**
Our app may use third-party services (such as maps or analytics tools) that may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

**9. Children’s Privacy**
